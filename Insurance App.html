Problems

Insurance partner companies do not necessarily have a consistent, overall picture currently of how their Medly based patients are performing. They use third-party industry accepted based trackers such as Equipp to estimate our performance. Inversely, Medly has to rely on more manual methods, or feel subject to being judged on these other third-party platforms, to report improvements on adherence rates and work to incrementally better our mutual patient/customers health performance.


Medly Pharmacy needs a way to illustrate to insurance partners that our mutual customers/patients receive more optimal pharmaceutical care and therefore better results saving them time and money. Healthier patients at the end of the day cost the insurance companies less money.

Audiences

The provider's app is deisigned for the insurance companies who are partnering with Medly.

  • 1. Internal Medly users: Insurance Partner manager is a role who focuses on insurance company statistics, and coordinates with insurance company's request in Medly.
  • 2. Insurance company employees: who are responsible to track the health data for the patients sent to Medly.
